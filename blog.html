Nandi Hills, also known as Nandidurg or Nandi Betta, is a popular hill station and tourist destination located near the city of Bangalore in the Indian state of Karnataka. It is situated approximately 60 kilometers (37 miles) north of Bangalore and is easily accessible by road. The hill station's elevation is around 1,478 meters (4,849 feet) above sea level, offering a cool and refreshing climate.

Goa is a state located on the western coast of India, known for its beautiful beaches, vibrant nightlife, and rich cultural heritage.Goa is situated on the Konkan coast, along the Arabian Sea. It is bordered by the states of Maharashtra to the north and Karnataka to the east and south.Goa is a popular tourist destination, both for domestic and international travelers.

VAGAMON

See more ideas about Travel

Vagamon is a popular hill station located in the Idukki district of the southern Indian state of Kerala. It's known for its breathtaking natural beauty, lush green landscapes, and cool climate, making it a favored destination for tourists seeking a serene and tranquil getaway.Vagamon is renowned for its stunning vistas, encompassing rolling hills, tea plantations, dense forests, and meadows.
